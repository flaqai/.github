## One Platform for the Next Generation of AI Products

[Flaq AI](https://flaq.ai/) is a global AI media generation platform that gives developers, creators, and product teams streamlined access to advanced **AI image generation, AI video generation, and large language models**. Instead of rebuilding integrations for every provider, teams can explore powerful models and connect production workflows through one unified API platform.

From creative experiments to scalable AI applications, Flaq AI is designed to shorten the distance between an idea and a working product. Our platform combines accessible AI tools, stable API workflows, and a growing model ecosystem so builders can spend less time managing infrastructure and more time creating experiences people value.

## Why We Invest in Open Source

Great AI products are built on shared knowledge. The Flaq AI team open-sources the workflows, research, and foundations we have refined through real product development—not as isolated demos, but as practical resources that other teams can study, adapt, and improve.

Our open-source work follows three principles:

- **Make AI development more accessible.** Useful starting points should be available to independent developers, startups, and growing teams—not only large organizations.
- **Turn experience into reusable systems.** We document repeatable approaches to AI agent skills, SEO content workflows, and SaaS product engineering so others do not need to begin from zero.
- **Build with the community.** Open collaboration produces stronger tools, clearer standards, and better ideas than closed development alone.

Every repository below represents time spent researching, curating, documenting, testing, translating, and maintaining resources for the wider AI builder community.

## Our Open-Source Projects

### [Awesome SEO Writing Skill](https://github.com/flaqai/Awesome_SEO_Writing_Skill)

A reusable, agent-ready workflow that turns a content brief into a complete SEO production process: search-intent planning, outlining, writing, fact-checking, image packaging, SEO auditing, and final file delivery. It distills nearly two years of hands-on content and publishing experience into a practical skill for Codex, Claude Code, and other AI coding agents.

**Best for:** SEO articles, product comparisons, tutorials, thought leadership, editorial content, and repeatable content operations.

### [Awesome Claude Code Skills](https://github.com/flaqai/awesome_claude_code_skills)

A curated, multilingual collection of practical Claude Code skills for software development, creative media, browser automation, documents, data visualization, marketing, and industry workflows. The project helps users discover useful skills faster while providing source information, usage context, and safety notes.

**Best for:** developers and teams looking to extend Claude Code with proven, task-specific workflows.

### [Awesome Codex Skills](https://github.com/flaqai/awesome_codex_skills)

A curated, multilingual directory of Codex skills spanning agent tooling, coding, frontend development, testing, media creation, documents, data, growth, security, and more. Alongside skill discovery, it includes guidance for creating, adapting, and installing custom skills.

**Best for:** Codex users who want to move from general-purpose assistance to reliable, reusable agent workflows.

### [Flaq SaaS Template](https://github.com/flaqai/flaq-saas-template)

A free, open-source foundation for launching AI-powered image and video generation products with the Flaq AI API. Built with Next.js, TypeScript, React, Tailwind CSS, internationalization, Cloudflare R2 storage, and production-focused SEO features, it includes working flows for text-to-image, image-to-image, text-to-video, image-to-video, and virtual try-on experiences.

**Best for:** founders and developers building an AI SaaS product, creative platform, model showcase, or AIGC startup.
